# EduPath

## What it is

EduPath is an AI-driven approach to generate personalized learning programmes based on individual goals.

It started as a real use case and is evolving into a more structured and repeatable system.

---

## Problem

Traditional learning paths have multiple limitations:

- High cost (masters, structured programmes, etc.)
- Fixed and rigid structures that don’t adapt to individuals
- Lack of personalization
- Too much content and no clear prioritization
- Difficulty in combining different formats (courses, videos, articles, etc.)

As a result, learning is often inefficient, expensive or hard to follow.

---

## Solution

EduPath uses AI to create flexible and personalized learning programmes.

It allows:

- Fully personalized learning paths based on specific goals  
- Dynamic structure that can evolve over time  
- Ability to go deep into specific topics or skip irrelevant ones  
- Integration of different content formats into a single structured path  
- Potential to create high-quality learning programmes at very low cost (even free)  

The focus is not just on generating content, but on making it usable and easy to follow.
