# WorldCup App — defining the go-to-market

## What I did

Started framing the World Cup app as a commercial product instead of just an internal project.

Identified the core positioning:
a branded team-building experience for companies during the World Cup.

Defined the first commercial angle around speed, customization and low-friction setup.

## What I learned

The value is not the prediction tool itself.

The value is turning a simple internal activity into a shared company experience.

Positioning matters:
this is stronger as a team-building product than as just a “pool app”.
