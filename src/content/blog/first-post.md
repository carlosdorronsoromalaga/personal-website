Welcome. This is the first post on my blog, and honestly, I'm writing it with a lot of excitement.

I'd wanted a space of my own for a while. Not another social feed, not a showcase, but a place where I can share how I think, what I work on, and the things that grab me enough to start building them. If you've made it this far, that's exactly what I'm hoping for: that you get to know me a little better.

I work in AI consulting and digital transformation, and in my own time I build products from scratch. Between the two, I'm constantly learning, getting things wrong, and turning over how technology changes (or doesn't change) the way we work and live. Some of that will land here, with no schedule and no fixed format. Just whatever I think is worth sharing.

But above all, I want this to be an invitation. If something you read here resonates, or you just feel like talking, reach out. I'm always open to new conversations, ideas, and opportunities. I genuinely believe a good conversation almost always leads to something: a project, an idea, a perspective you hadn't considered, or simply good company.

So thanks for stopping by. Let's see where this goes.
